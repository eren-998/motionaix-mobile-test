A. Render Performance Overhaul & Latency Diagnosis:
   - Root Cause Identified: html-to-image DOM-to-SVG-to-Canvas serialization was running on every frame.
   - Optimization #1: Configured VP9 WebCodecs VideoEncoder to `latencyMode: "realtime"` (3-5x faster single-pass encoding).

   - Optimization #3: Implemented `ExportModeContext` to scale particle counts (150 -> 45 in FireSlider) during video export.
   - Optimization #4: Batched React progress state updates to ~20 key intervals instead of re-rendering on every single frame.
